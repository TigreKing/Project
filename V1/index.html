Dedication

This website is dedicated to the tragedy of Darth Plagueis the Wise, a story the Jedi would not tell you.

Purpose Statement

The Star Wars universe first entered the scene 43 years ago with the release of the first film, at the time simply titled Star Wars but know known as A New Hope. In the ensuing years Star Wars has cemented itself as a global pop-cultural icon that has touched the lives of millions. Despite this, most people have failed to understand that the Empire in general and Emperor Palpatine in particular are not actually villains but rather the true unsung heros of the saga. I've created this website to correct the record on Palpatine. My hope is that I can put to an end the shameful slander his character has undergone and spread the true light of Palpatines greatness to the masses.

Palpatine Pre-Empire

Despite eventually becoming the man who brought the galaxy into a new age of peace, prosperity, and security by bringing an end to the corrupt and decadent Old Republic, surprisingly little is known about the origins of palpatine. Because of this, he is misunderstood by many due to years of propoganda purpetrated by the terrorist insurgent group known as the Rebel Alliance. My goal with this page is to shed some light on Palpatine's life before becoming Emperor, what inspired him to acheive what he did, and to hopefully set the record straight about this great man.

Palpatine Post-Empire

After finally destroying the corrupt so called 'Republic' Palpatine continued to dedicate his life towards the betterment of all galactic citizens who he viewed like his dear children. Unfortunately in the years since he was assassinated by the cowardly murderer Anakin Skywalker, the Rebels have worked tireless to erase his legacy by painting him as some sort of monster. As such, this page will be dedicated to providing a true, unbiased account of his many accomplishments as Emperor.

Notable Quotes

Like many great men, Palpatine has throughout his life uttered many great sayings and dispenced incredible wisdom to those who earn for truth. This page is intended to chronicle such notable quotes to preserve them for posterity that they may continue to provide hope and inspiration for many years to come.
